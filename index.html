ABOUT ME

  • How I started

    September 2017, I began studying BSc Economics at the University of Leeds.

  • Tech Renaissance

    During an innovation module, I had the idea to create a virtual queueing app for barbershops app. It was here I started to realise the power of technology-driven solutions.

    Despite my lack of tech skills at the time, I used no-code tools to develop the idea into an MVP.

  • Economist turned Technologist

    2020, I secured a placement at the Office for National Statistics, as a student economist.

    Here, I learnt to code in Python for Data Analysis. I really enjoyed it, more so than writing Economic analysis (no hard feelings ONS!)

    I grew interest in solving real problems using technology. In completing my placement, I decided to become a Software Engineer.

  • Tech Soft Life

    I finished my Economics degree in June 2022, and started working as a Software Engineer since.

    And trust me, I’m loving it!
